About Cock Ro Ko

Cock Ro Ko is a premier dining destination specializing in grilled meats, BBQ, and contemporary cuisine. Our restaurant offers a warm, inviting atmosphere perfect for family gatherings, romantic dinners, and group celebrations.

Founded in 2010, we take pride in using only the freshest ingredients sourced from local farmers and suppliers. Our chefs bring years of expertise to create memorable dining experiences.

Award-Winning Cuisine

Recognized as "Best BBQ Restaurant" for three consecutive years by Food & Dining Magazine.
